Guiding Framework

Vision, Mission, and Core Values

The principles below provide direction, tone, and institutional meaning to learner leadership within Gumaca National High School.

Organizational Framework

Purpose and standards that shape SSLG leadership

These statements guide the organization’s planning, conduct, programs, and relationship with the learner body and school community.

Vision

An empowered, disciplined, and service-driven learner community

To help build a learner community that is united, responsible, participatory, and committed to excellence in school life and public-minded leadership.

Mission

Lead, represent, and serve with integrity

To represent learners responsibly, implement meaningful student programs, and strengthen school participation through organized service, accountability, and respectful leadership.

Core Values

The values that define the SSLG’s institutional tone

Each value contributes to a leadership culture that is both school-appropriate and service-oriented.

I

Integrity

Acting honestly, consistently, and responsibly in both words and actions.

A

Accountability

Taking ownership of leadership decisions, commitments, and organizational responsibilities.

R

Respect

Valuing every learner, stakeholder, and school process through discipline and professionalism.

S

Service

Keeping learner welfare, school needs, and shared responsibilities at the heart of leadership.

U

Unity

Encouraging collaboration, participation, and collective school spirit among learners.

E

Excellence

Pursuing quality, discipline, clarity, and thoughtful execution in every initiative.
