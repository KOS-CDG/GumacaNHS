About the SSLG

The recognized learner leadership body of the school

The Supreme Secondary Learner Government serves as the organized student leadership body of Gumaca National High School. It helps represent learner concerns, support school programs, and build a more participatory and responsible campus community.

Institutional Direction

Service, representation, and organized student participation

The SSLG connects leadership with actual school service through coordination, communication, consultation, and learner-centered programs.

Guiding Principles

Vision, Mission, and Core Values

The SSLG is guided by principles that promote integrity, accountability, service, school pride, and inclusive student leadership.

Vision

An empowered learner community

To help cultivate disciplined, service-driven, and participatory learners who contribute positively to school life and community.

Mission

Responsible student representation

To lead with integrity, organize meaningful programs, and uphold student welfare through accountable service and active campus engagement.

Core Values

Leadership shaped by principle

  • Integrity and accountability
  • Service and compassion
  • Respect and inclusivity
  • Leadership and excellence
  • Unity and participation
